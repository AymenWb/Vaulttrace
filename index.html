About the Project

Vaulttrace is a powerful blockchain explorer built to find lost, dormant, and forgotten cryptocurrency wallets. It analyzes on-chain activity to highlight addresses with large balances and long inactivity periods.

Key Features

  • Scan thousands of addresses across multiple blockchains
  • Filter by inactivity duration, wallet balance, or transaction history
  • Export results to CSV or JSON
  • Simple dashboard (coming soon)

Why Vaulttrace?

It's estimated that billions in crypto lie untouched in forgotten wallets. Vaulttrace empowers digital archaeologists, security experts, and crypto enthusiasts to locate and analyze these valuable addresses.
